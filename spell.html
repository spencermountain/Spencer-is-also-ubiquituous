this app spell-checks a word in Aspell from ubiquity.

GNU Aspell is a Free and Open Source spell checker. Its main feature is that it does a superior job of suggesting possible replacements for a misspelled word than just about any other spell checker out there for the English language. Aspell can also easily check documents in UTF-8 without having to use a special dictionary. Aspell will also do its best to respect the current locale setting, and has support for using multiple dictionaries at once.

Usage: spell bananna
